# Thorek the Blacksmith - Game Statistics

## BASIC STATS
**Race:** Mountain Dwarf
**Class:** Expert/Artisan
**Level:** 5
**Armor Class:** 16 (Chain Mail)
**Hit Points:** 45
**Speed:** 25 feet (not reduced by armor)

## ABILITY SCORES
- **Strength:** 18 (+4)
- **Dexterity:** 10 (+0)
- **Constitution:** 16 (+3)
- **Intelligence:** 14 (+2)
- **Wisdom:** 13 (+1)
- **Charisma:** 11 (+0)

## SKILLS
- **Athletics:** +7
- **History:** +5 (especially metalworking and dwarven lore)
- **Insight:** +4
- **Investigation:** +5
- **Smith's Tools:** +8 (expertise)

## RACIAL TRAITS
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Dwarven Resilience:** Advantage against poison
- **Stonecunning:** Double proficiency on stone-related History checks

## COMBAT ABILITIES
**Actions:**
- **Warhammer:** +7 to hit, 1d8+4 bludgeoning damage (1d10+4 two-handed)
- **Light Hammer (thrown):** +7 to hit, 1d4+4 bludgeoning damage

**Special:** 
- **Forge Master:** Can quickly repair damaged equipment during short rests
- **Tactical Fighter:** Uses environment to advantage (anvils, hot metal, etc.)

## NON-COMBAT ABILITIES
- **Master Craftsman:** Can upgrade weapons and armor given time and materials
- **Metallurgy Expert:** Can identify magical metals and their properties
- **Ancient Knowledge:** Knows dwarven history and traditional monster-fighting techniques
