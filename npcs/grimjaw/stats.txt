# Grimjaw the Goblin Chieftain - Game Statistics

## BASIC STATS
**Race:** Goblin (Large for his kind)
**Class:** Barbarian/Chieftain
**Level:** 4
**Armor Class:** 15 (Studded Leather + Dex + Natural Armor)
**Hit Points:** 45
**Speed:** 30 feet

## ABILITY SCORES
- **Strength:** 16 (+3)
- **Dexterity:** 16 (+3)
- **Constitution:** 15 (+2)
- **Intelligence:** 12 (+1)
- **Wisdom:** 13 (+1)
- **Charisma:** 14 (+2)

## SKILLS
- **Athletics:** +5
- **Intimidation:** +4
- **Perception:** +3
- **Stealth:** +5
- **Survival:** +3

## RACIAL TRAITS
- **Darkvision:** 60 feet
- **Nimble Escape:** Can Disengage or Hide as bonus action
- **Small Size Advantage:** Can move through larger creature spaces

## COMBAT ABILITIES
**Actions:**
- **Scimitar:** +6 to hit, 1d6+3 slashing damage
- **Shortbow:** +6 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing damage (range 80/320)
- **Leadership (1/day):** All allied goblins within 30 feet gain advantage on next attack

**Bonus Actions:**
- **Rage (2/day):** +2 damage, resistance to physical damage for 1 minute
- **Nimble Escape:** Disengage or Hide

## TACTICS
- Uses terrain advantage and goblin numbers
- Focuses fire on obvious threats (heavy armor, spellcasters)
- Retreats if reduced to 15 HP or fewer
- Calls for parley if clearly outmatched

## GOBLIN WARBAND (If Combat Occurs)
- **4-6 Goblin Warriors** (AC 13, HP 7, +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage)
- **2 Goblin Archers** (AC 13, HP 7, +4 to hit, 1d6+2 damage, range)
- **1 Goblin Shaman** (AC 12, HP 9, can cast healing word and magic missile)
